# SVS — What's Next: Launch & SEO Plan

**Updated:** 7 July 2026 · The website is rebuilt and ready. This is the order to do things in.

---

## Phase 1 — Before launch (this week)

The site works today, but three things make it *sell*:

1. **Add real photos.** 6–12 portfolio images (portrait 4:5, ~1200px, under 300KB each — optimize at squoosh.app) plus 1 hero image. Follow the comments inside `index.html` — each tile has a marked slot.
2. **Fix the placeholders.** Search "EDIT" in `index.html`: real stat numbers and at least 2 real testimonials (ask past clients on WhatsApp — one sentence is enough).
3. **Confirm the config.** Open `js/site.js`, check the CFG block at the top: Pixieset URL, WhatsApp, email, socials. One-line edits.

## Phase 2 — Launch (one afternoon)

1. **Host it free:** drag the `Website` folder onto **netlify.com/drop**. You get a live URL instantly.
2. **Custom domain (recommended, ~GHS 150–300/yr):** buy `selasievisualstudio.com`, connect it in Netlify (Domains → add). A real domain doubles trust and is required for serious SEO.
3. Click every button on your phone and a PC once live.

## Phase 3 — SEO foundation (week 1 after launch)

Goal: rank for "photographer Accra", "wedding photographer Accra", "food photographer Accra".

1. **Google Business Profile is your #1 weapon.** You already have one — now: set the website link, add 15–20 best photos, correct categories (Photographer / Videographer / Wedding photographer), service area Greater Accra, hours, and the exact phrase "photography and videography in Accra, Ghana" in the description.
2. **Google Search Console** (free): verify your domain, submit `sitemap.xml`. This tells Google you exist — otherwise you wait months to be found.
3. **Reviews engine:** after every delivered gallery, send the WhatsApp review ask (the dashboard's 💬 button already does this). 10+ Google reviews with the word "photographer" in them outranks most competitors locally.

## Phase 4 — Ongoing SEO & content (weekly, 30 min)

1. **Post to Google Business Profile weekly** — one photo + one line ("Corporate headshots for [client] in Osu"). GBP posts are the most underused local-SEO lever in Ghana.
2. **Instagram/TikTok bio links → website**, and mention the site verbally in reels. Social signals drive the branded searches that lift rankings.
3. **Name your image files before uploading** to the site: `wedding-photographer-accra-01.jpg`, not `IMG_2041.jpg`, and write honest alt text. Already wired to matter — the site's structure (JSON-LD, meta, sitemap) is done.
4. **Once a month, add one portfolio tile** from your best recent shoot. Fresh content = crawl frequency.

## Phase 5 — Measure & compound (monthly)

- Search Console: which queries show your site → double down on those words in your GBP posts.
- Ask every new client **"how did you find us?"** — log it in the dashboard client notes.
- When galleries deliver, Pixieset traffic → site → more branded search. The loop feeds itself.
