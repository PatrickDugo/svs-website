The studio

Considered, unhurried imagery

We're drawn to the moments you'd otherwise forget — the glance, the gathering, the small detail — and we shape them into images that feel as alive as the day they happened.

Founded by Patrick Selasie Dugo and built as a collaborative practice, Selasie Visual Studio works closely with you before a single frame is made: your story, your pace, your light.

Every gallery is edited in our signature style, backed up three times over, and delivered privately — so what matters to you is kept beautifully, and kept safe.
