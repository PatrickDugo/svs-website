Optimise & place photos

Shrinks files for fast loading and strips hidden location/camera data. Choose where they belong, drop one photo or a whole gallery, and download them ready to upload. Your copyright stays yours.

◳
Drop photos here, or click to choose
One or a whole gallery at once · JPG or PNG · nothing leaves your computer

Two ways to send them to GitHub

Download all — saves each photo to your Downloads. On GitHub, open that folder and drag them all in at once. Best for updating hero, about, services, or adding to a gallery that already exists.

Download folder (.zip) — saves the whole batch as a folder. Unzip it, then drag that folder onto GitHub's upload page — it creates the gallery folder for you. Best for a brand-new gallery.

Publish your changes

Download your edited text, then drag it into GitHub. That's the whole publish step.
